What is Highkey and Lowkey?

Highkey
High key photography uses unnaturally bright lighting to blow out most or all harsh shadows in an image.
Lowkey
A low-key image is one that contains predominantly dark tones and colours. Like high-key images, they convey atmosphere and mood
